1. Information We Collect

Pulsar collects the following non-personally identifiable information automatically:

The collected data is used exclusively for:

Data is stored securely. We do not sell or share any user data with third parties, except as required by law.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data stored by Pulsar. To do so, please contact us via Discord.
